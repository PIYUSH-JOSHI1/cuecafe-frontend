Refund Policy

We want you to have a great experience at Cue Stories. If you need to cancel your booking, please review our refund policy below.

Cancellation & Refunds

  • Bookings cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Bookings cancelled within 24 hours of the scheduled time are not eligible for a refund.
  • Refunds (if applicable) will be processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at info@cuestories.com with your booking details.

Exceptions

  • Refunds are not provided for no-shows or late arrivals.
  • Special events or offers may have separate refund policies.
