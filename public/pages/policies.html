Our Policies

Please review our booking, cancellation, and club policies

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations More Than 24 Hours Before Slot

Full refund will be processed to your original payment method within 3-5 business days.

Cancellations Within 24 Hours of Slot

A cancellation fee of 10% of the booking amount will be deducted. The remaining amount will be refunded.

No-Show (Not Cancelled)

The full amount will be forfeited. No refund will be issued for no-shows.

Important: Cancellation requests must be made through the app or by calling 8408068388.

Rescheduling Policy

Rescheduling of booked slots is NOT allowed by club policy.

If you need to change your slot, you must cancel the current booking (following cancellation policy) and book a new slot separately.

Club Rules & Guidelines

Dress Code
  • Non-marking shoes are COMPULSORY
  • Regular sneakers or formal shoes are fine
  • Avoid shoes with colored soles or dark rubber soles
Code of Conduct
  • Respect all other players and staff
  • Maintain the tables and equipment with care
  • No abusive language or behavior
  • Keep noise levels reasonable
Food & Beverages
  • No outside food and beverages allowed
  • Ask staff for available food options
Equipment Care
  • Use cues and equipment responsibly
  • Report any damage immediately
  • Damages caused by negligence may incur charges

Booking Terms & Conditions

Age Policy

Players must be 13 years or older. Parents/guardians must be present for players under 18 years.

Booking Confirmation

Booking confirmation is received after successful payment. You will receive an email/SMS with booking details.

Slot Duration

Each slot is 1 hour. You must vacate the table on time for the next booking.

Late Arrivals

If you're more than 15 minutes late, we may release the slot for other bookings. No refund will be given.

Equipment Damage

Customers are responsible for any damage caused to tables or equipment due to negligence. Damages will be billed accordingly.

Refund Policy

  • Cancellation Refunds: Processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method
  • Payment Failures: If your payment failed but was debited, please contact us immediately
  • Disputes: Please contact support@cuestories.com with proof for dispute resolution
  • No-Shows: No refunds for no-shows or missed bookings

Privacy & Data Protection

  • Your personal data is encrypted and secured
  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • Payment information is handled by Razorpay securely
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties without consent

Liability & Safety

Cue Stories is not responsible for:

  • Personal injuries during play (players play at their own risk)
  • Loss or theft of personal belongings
  • Server downtime or booking system failures

Players are advised to follow all safety guidelines and use equipment properly.
